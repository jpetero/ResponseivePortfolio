BIO

I am Julius Peter Oketayot. I graduated from Kyambogo University Kampala with a Bachelor of Science in Chemical Engineering. Later I got a job as a Production Manager at Peacock Paints Limited in Kampala (Oct - 2018) but since then I had a deep love of becoming a software developer and I was looking for any available to join the software industry 100%

I looked for opportunity around but Kampala and also online but I could not even find because the companies need people with enough skills and from computer related background. Not untill then when my luck came my way and I was admitted at Refactory Uganda (Industry's blueprint for hiring developers) for a short course which got me into the 6 months bootcamp.

I had to quit my job at the Paints Factory and took the opportunity seriously to become a senior software developer. Currently I have developed my skills a lot Which I would love to share with you below;

Backend Developer in Dotnet

Comprehensive C# Knowledge (Reflection, Lambda, LINQ, Multihreading etc.), Web service implementation (ASP.NET Core MVC), Strong Object-Oriented development skills, Understanding of general web architecture, SQL server and LINQ experience, Serialization (JSON, XML) etc.

Frontend Developer in Angular

Deep understanding of Angular single page application, Components, Data Binding (interpolation), Pipes etc. And aslo other frontend technologies like HTML, Bootstrap 4, CSS, SASS, JavaScript, Web pack, Ajax, Jquery etc.

Project Manager

Working together with large team members, team coordination, Agile methodologies and principles, Version control with Git, User stories, backlogs and stories mapping with Trello and Jira etc.
